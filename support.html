Frequently Asked Questions

How do I analyze a video?

Simply paste a YouTube video URL into the app and tap "Analyze". Video Notes will generate a summary and key notes from the video content.

Where are my notes stored?

All your video notes and history are stored locally on your device. They are never uploaded to our servers and remain private to you.

Do I need an account?

No. Video Notes does not require registration or login. You can start using the app immediately.

What videos are supported?

Video Notes currently supports YouTube videos that have available transcripts or captions.

How do I delete my data?

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

Is my data private?

Yes. We do not collect personal information or link any data to your identity. Please see our Privacy Policy for details.

Troubleshooting

The app won't analyze my video

Make sure the video has available captions or transcripts. Some videos may not have transcripts available, which prevents analysis.

Analysis is taking too long

Longer videos may take more time to process. Please ensure you have a stable internet connection and try again.
