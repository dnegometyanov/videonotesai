Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Video Notes ("we," "our," or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. Video Notes is developed by Denys Nehometianov.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This policy complies with applicable privacy laws including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Data Controller

Denys Nehometianov
Email: videonotes.ai@gmail.com

Information We Collect

Device Identifier

We collect a Device Identifier through Firebase App Check, a service provided by Google. This identifier is used solely for:

  • App Functionality: Securing API communications and preventing unauthorized access to our services

This device identifier:

  • Is not linked to your personal identity
  • Is not used for tracking you across other apps or websites
  • Is not shared with advertisers or data brokers

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable information
  • User Accounts: The app does not require or support user accounts
  • Video History: Your analyzed videos and notes are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to our servers
  • Location Data: We do not collect your location
  • Contacts: We do not access your contacts
  • Photos or Media: We do not access your photo library

How We Use Your Information

The device identifier collected via Firebase App Check is used exclusively for:

  1. API Security: Verifying that requests to our services come from legitimate instances of the app
  2. Fraud Prevention: Protecting against abuse and unauthorized access

Third-Party Services

Firebase App Check (Google)

We use Firebase App Check to verify the authenticity of app instances. Firebase App Check may collect:

  • Device attestation tokens
  • App instance identifiers

For more information about Firebase's data practices, please review:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

AI Content Generation

Video Notes uses artificial intelligence to generate video summaries and notes. The AI-generated content:

  • Is created based on the video content you choose to analyze
  • Is processed through secure API connections
  • May be cached on our servers to improve performance, but without any user, device, or app identifiers ‚Äî meaning we cannot link summaries to any individual user

Data Retention

  • Device Identifier: Retained only as long as necessary for security verification purposes
  • Local Data: Video notes and history stored on your device remain there until you delete the app or clear the app's data

Your Rights

For All Users

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about what data we have about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of any data we have collected
  • Opt-out: Uninstall the app to stop all data collection

For European Union Residents (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you additionally have the right to:

  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing
  • Object: Object to processing of your data

For California Residents (CCPA)

Under CCPA, you have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Equal service and price, even if you exercise your privacy rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at videonotes.ai@gmail.com.
