2. What This Policy Covers

This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your choices. It applies to the Video Notes iOS application.

We have designed this app with privacy in mind. We do not create user accounts, we do not collect personal identifiers, and we do not track you across apps or websites.

3. Information We Collect and Process

3.1 Device Attestation Token

We use Firebase App Check, a service provided by Google, to verify that requests to our servers come from genuine instances of the app. Firebase App Check generates a device attestation token for this purpose.

What it is: A security token used to prevent abuse of our service.

What it is not: This token is not linked to your identity, is not used for tracking, and is not shared with advertisers.

Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)): Legitimate interest in protecting our service from abuse and unauthorized access.

3.2 Summaries Are Not Linked to You

When you request a summary, we generate it using the content needed to fulfill your request and may use an AI service provider to produce the summary. We may store (cache) generated summaries on our server to improve performance for future requests for the same content.

We do not store any user identifiers on our servers and we do not include user identifiers in requests to the AI service provider. As a result, cached summaries are not linked to you or your device.

Note on transcript content: Transcripts are created by third parties (video uploaders). In some cases, transcript text may incidentally include personal data (for example, names mentioned in the video). We do not control this content. The AI service provider processes the submitted text only to generate the summary, under its applicable contractual terms and service configuration.

Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)): Performance of a contract â€” providing the summarization service you requested.

3.3 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Your personal notes, saved summaries, and viewing history are stored locally on your device only. This locally stored data is not transmitted to our servers. You have full control over it and can delete it at any time by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

3.4 Information We Do Not Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos, media, or files
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Browsing history or activity outside this app

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above for these purposes only:

Device attestation token: To verify that requests come from legitimate app instances and to prevent abuse.

Video link and transcript text: To generate the summary you requested and, where applicable, to cache the result for performance.

We do not use any data for advertising, profiling, or tracking.

5. Service Providers (Recipients)

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

5.1 Firebase App Check (Google)

Purpose: App attestation and abuse prevention.

Data processed: Device attestation tokens.

Role: Data processor acting on our instructions.

5.2 AI Service Provider

Purpose: Generate text summaries from video transcripts.

Data processed: Transcript text. No user identifiers are included in requests. To the extent transcript text incidentally contains personal data (e.g., names mentioned in the video), the AI provider processes this as a data processor on our behalf.

Role: Data processor, to the extent personal data is processed.

We maintain appropriate contractual arrangements with our service providers requiring them to protect any data they process on our behalf and to process it only according to our instructions.

6. Data Retention

Device attestation tokens: Retained only for the duration needed to validate a request. No long-term storage.

Cached summaries: Generated summaries may be cached on our server to improve performance. This cache uses a rolling expiration policy with limited retention. Cached summaries do not contain user identifiers and cannot be linked to any individual.

Local data: Data stored on your device (notes, saved summaries, history) remains until you delete the app or clear the app data.

7. International Data Transfers

Our service providers may process data in countries outside your own, including countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA). Where personal data is transferred outside the EEA, we rely on appropriate safeguards recognized under GDPR, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, or transfers to countries recognized as providing an adequate level of data protection.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

Under GDPR, you have rights regarding your personal data, including the rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection.

How our design affects these rights

Because we do not collect user identifiers and do not link server-side data to individuals, we are generally unable to identify which cached data, if any, relates to you. Under GDPR Article 11, where processing does not require identification of the data subject, we are not obligated to maintain or acquire additional information solely to identify you. This means:

  • If you contact us with a rights request, we may not be able to locate your data unless you provide additional information that enables us to do so.
  • For locally stored data (on your device), you can exercise your rights directly by viewing, exporting, or deleting that data through your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

What you can do

  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.
  • Stop all future data processing by uninstalling the app.
  • Contact us with questions or requests at videonotes.ai@gmail.com. If you believe specific cached data relates to you and can provide information to help us identify it, we will make reasonable efforts to assist.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority if you are in the EU.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not share data with advertisers or data brokers.
  • We do not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

9. Is Providing Data Mandatory?

Providing the video link is necessary to use the summarization feature. If you do not provide a link, we cannot generate a summary for you. The device attestation token is generated automatically to secure the service; without it, requests may be rejected to prevent abuse.
