Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

Introduction

Video Notes is a mobile app that helps you summarize YouTube videos. This policy explains how we handle your information. We've designed the app with privacy in mind — we don't create accounts, don't track you, and keep your data local.

Contact: videonotes.ai@gmail.com

Information We Collect

  • Device attestation token — A security token from Firebase App Check to verify requests come from the real app (not linked to your identity)
  • Video transcripts — Sent to our AI service to generate summaries (not linked to you)
  • Cached summaries — We may cache summaries on our server to speed up future requests for the same video

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No accounts or login
  • No names, emails, or phone numbers
  • No location data
  • No contacts or photos
  • No advertising IDs
  • No cross-app tracking

How We Use Your Information

  • Device token — To prevent abuse and verify legitimate app requests
  • Transcripts — To generate the video summary you requested
  • Caching — To improve performance for popular videos

We never use your data for advertising, profiling, or tracking.

Third-Party Services

Firebase App Check (Google) — Verifies that requests come from the genuine app. Firebase Privacy

AI Service Provider — Generates summaries from video transcripts. No user identifiers are sent.

Data Retention

  • Device tokens are temporary and not stored long-term
  • Cached summaries are anonymous and cannot be linked to you
  • Local data (your notes and history) stays on your device until you delete it

Your Rights

  • Delete your data — Uninstall the app or clear app data in device settings
  • Stop processing — Uninstalling the app stops all future data processing
  • Questions — Contact us at videonotes.ai@gmail.com

We do not sell personal information or share data with advertisers.

Security

We use encrypted connections (HTTPS) and Firebase App Check to protect the service. Your notes and history are stored only on your device.

Children

Video Notes is for users 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16.

Changes

We may update this policy and will change the "Last Updated" date. For significant changes, we'll notify you in the app.
