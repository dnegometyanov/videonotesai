Information We Collect

Device Identifier

We collect a Device Identifier through Firebase App Check, a service provided by Google. This identifier is used solely for:

  • App Functionality: Securing API communications and preventing unauthorized access to our services

This device identifier:

  • Is not linked to your personal identity
  • Is not used for tracking you across other apps or websites
  • Is not shared with advertisers or data brokers

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable information
  • User Accounts: The app does not require or support user accounts
  • Video History: Your analyzed videos and notes are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to our servers
  • Location Data: We do not collect your location
  • Contacts: We do not access your contacts
  • Photos or Media: We do not access your photo library

How We Use Your Information

The device identifier collected via Firebase App Check is used exclusively for:

  1. API Security: Verifying that requests to our services come from legitimate instances of the app
  2. Fraud Prevention: Protecting against abuse and unauthorized access

Third-Party Services

Firebase App Check (Google)

We use Firebase App Check to verify the authenticity of app instances. Firebase App Check may collect:

  • Device attestation tokens
  • App instance identifiers

For more information about Firebase's data practices, please review:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

AI Content Generation

Video Notes uses artificial intelligence to generate video summaries and notes. When you analyze a video:

  • The video URL (not the video itself) is sent to our servers
  • Our servers fetch the video transcript and send it to an AI service for processing
  • The generated summary may be cached on our servers to improve performance for future requests
  • Cached summaries are stored without any user, device, or app identifiers — meaning we cannot link them to any individual user
  • No video content is stored on our servers; only the generated text summaries may be cached

Data Retention

  • Device Identifier: Retained only as long as necessary for security verification purposes
  • Local Data: Video notes and history stored on your device remain there until you delete the app or clear the app's data

Your Rights

Your Legal Rights

Under GDPR (for EU residents), CCPA (for California residents), and other privacy laws, you may have rights including access to your data, deletion, rectification, portability, and the ability to object to processing.

Important Limitation

We want to be transparent: most individual data rights are difficult to exercise in practice because of how we've designed the app to protect your privacy:

  • No user accounts exist — we have no way to identify you
  • Cached summaries are stored anonymously — without any user, device, or app identifiers
  • We cannot verify your identity — we have no email, name, or account to match
  • We cannot link server-side data to individuals — the data simply isn't connected to any person

This design choice was intentional to maximize your privacy. We do not associate processed video summaries with any personally identifiable user. All processing is performed without linking data to an individual identity, which means we cannot fulfill requests like "show me my data" or "delete my data" for server-cached summaries.

  • Stop data collection: Uninstall the app to stop all future data collection
  • Contact us: If you have questions or concerns, email us at videonotes.ai@gmail.com
  • Lodge a complaint: EU residents may file a complaint with their local data protection supervisory authority

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers
  • We provide equal service regardless of your privacy choices
